Lead Software Engineer

As a Lead Software Engineer, you will work within a cross functional team with a devops mindset to deliver large, distributed systems with low latency and high availability. You will be part of building up the team with the right people. You will guide them and coach them to become better developers and make sure the team delivers great quality software.

Responsibilities

  • Work with the team to build new features & functionality in an agile way
  • Responsible for the architecture and code quality for the product area that the team is responsible for.
  • Perform code and design reviews with the team to improve the quality
  • Coach other developers in the team to make sure the team continuously improve
  • Participate in the recruitment process for new software engineers
  • Work together with architects and delivery leads for other product teams to make sure all components work together in a great way
  • Build automated test to ensure great quality in the product
  • Ensure the performance and reliability of the system
  • Ensure service operability by implementing proper security, monitoring and alerting based on the Nasdaq Operating Environment
  • Update and create needed documentation
  • Aware of technology trends and decide which frameworks/technologies should be part of the solution

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or Mathematics
  • Experience in developing low latency applications
  • Experience working as a scrum master
  • Excellent analytical and problem solving skills
  • 4+ experience from Java development
  • Self-awareness: Self-motivated with an awareness with an awareness and understanding of one's strengths and areas of development.
  • Experience in deploying systems both in the cloud and on prem
